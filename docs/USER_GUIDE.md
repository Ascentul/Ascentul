## Getting Started

### Creating Your First Resume

1. **Sign In** to your account
2. **Navigate** to the Resume Builder
3. **Click** "Create New Resume" or start with a template
4. **Select** a template that matches your style
5. **Start editing** by clicking any section

### Understanding the Interface

**Header Bar:**
- AI Actions button - Access Generate, Tailor, and Tidy features
- Layout Settings - Customize spacing and appearance
- Export - Download your resume as PDF

**Main Canvas:**
- Your resume preview
- Click any block to edit
- Drag to reorder sections (coming soon)

**Right Sidebar (optional):**
- Resume Coach - Get expert tips
- Suggestions panel - See improvements

## AI Features

### 1. Generate Content 🪄

**What it does:** Creates resume content from your career profile based on a target role.

**How to use:**
1. Click "AI Actions" in the header
2. Select "Generate Content"
3. Enter your target job title (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
4. Optionally add target company
5. Click "Generate Resume"
6. Wait for AI to create your content
7. Review and edit as needed

**When to use:**
- Starting a new resume from scratch
- Applying for a specific role
- Tailoring content to industry

**Tips:**
- Be specific with job title
- Include target company for better customization
- Review generated content for accuracy
- Add your personal touch

### 2. Tailor to Job 🎯

**What it does:** Customizes your resume to match a specific job posting.

**How to use:**
1. Click "AI Actions" → "Tailor to Job"
2. Paste the job description
3. Click "Tailor Resume"
4. Review the before/after comparison
5. Click "Accept Changes" to apply

**When to use:**
- Applying for specific job postings
- Matching keywords in job description
- Optimizing for ATS systems

**Tips:**
- Copy entire job description
- Review all changes carefully
- Keep original version saved
- Customize further after tailoring

### 3. Tidy & Improve ✨

**What it does:** Enhances grammar, clarity, and impact of existing content.

**How to use:**
1. Click "AI Actions" → "Tidy & Improve"
2. Wait for processing (usually 5-10 seconds)
3. Review improvements
4. Undo if needed

**When to use:**
- Quick improvements before sending
- Fixing grammar and typos
- Strengthening action verbs
- Improving clarity

**Tips:**
- Use after making manual edits
- Can only tidy once every 20 seconds
- Review changes before finalizing
- Combines well with suggestions

**Cooldown:** 20 seconds between uses to prevent API overuse.

## Smart Suggestions

### What Are Suggestions?

Smart suggestions analyze your resume content and provide actionable tips to improve impact, clarity, and ATS compatibility.

### How to See Suggestions

1. Click any resume block (Experience, Summary, etc.)
2. Suggestions appear at the bottom of selected block
3. Each suggestion shows:
   - Priority level (High/Medium/Low)
   - Issue type (Verb, Metrics, Length, etc.)
   - Specific recommendation
   - Why it matters

### Suggestion Types

**High Priority (Red):**
- ❌ Weak action verbs → Use stronger alternatives
- ❌ Missing metrics → Add numbers and percentages
- ❌ Vague language → Be specific

**Medium Priority (Yellow):**
- ⚠️ Bullet too long → Keep under 150 characters
- ⚠️ Wrong tense → Current roles use present tense
- ⚠️ Missing keywords → Add relevant skills

**Low Priority (Blue):**
- ℹ️ Minor improvements
- ℹ️ Optional optimizations
- ℹ️ Style suggestions

### Managing Suggestions

**Dismiss:** Click the X button to hide a suggestion
- Dismissed suggestions won't appear again
- Stored in your browser

**Reset:** Clear browser data to see all suggestions again

## Resume Coach

### What is Resume Coach?

An expert advisor panel that provides resume writing best practices and tips.

### Coach Categories

**Content Tips:**
- Quantify achievements with metrics
- Use strong action verbs
- Show impact, not just duties

**Strategy Tips:**
- Tailor to target role
- Prioritize relevant experience
- Match job keywords

**Formatting Tips:**
- Keep to one page (unless 10+ years experience)
- Use consistent formatting
- Ensure readability

**ATS Optimization:**
- Avoid complex formatting
- Use standard section headings
- Include relevant keywords

### Using Coach Tips

1. Open Coach panel (if available)
2. Browse tips by category
3. Click category to expand
4. Read and apply tips
5. Dismiss tips as you complete them

## Tips & Best Practices

### Writing Strong Bullets

**❌ Weak:**
```
- Responsible for managing team projects
- Helped improve sales numbers
- Did various tasks
```

**✅ Strong:**
```
- Led cross-functional team of 8 to deliver 3 major projects on time
- Increased Q2 sales by 35% through strategic partnerships
- Optimized backend API reducing response time by 50%
```

**Formula:** Action Verb + What You Did + Quantifiable Result

### Quantifying Achievements

Always include:
- Numbers (team size, projects, users)
- Percentages (growth, improvement, efficiency)
- Time frames (deadlines met, speed improvements)
- Scale (millions of users, thousands of dollars)

**Examples:**
- ❌ "Improved website performance"
- ✅ "Improved website performance by 40%, reducing load time from 5s to 3s"

### ATS Optimization

**Do:**
- Use standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
- Include keywords from job description
- Use simple, clean formatting
- Save as PDF

**Don't:**
- Use tables or text boxes
- Include images or graphics
- Use unusual fonts
- Rely on colors for information

### Length Guidelines

**One Page:**
- 0-7 years of experience
- Recent graduates
- Career changers

**Two Pages:**
- 8+ years of experience
- Multiple relevant roles
- Extensive achievements

**Content Priority:**
1. Most recent, relevant experience
2. Quantifiable achievements
3. Relevant skills
4. Education (less important if experienced)

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How often is my resume saved?**
A: Automatically after each edit. No manual save needed.

**Q: Can I have multiple resumes?**
A: Yes! Create different versions for different roles.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely.

### AI Features

**Q: How many times can I use AI features?**
A: Generate and Tailor: Unlimited
Tidy: Once every 20 seconds (rate limit)

**Q: Do AI features cost credits?**
A: Check your subscription plan for limits.

**Q: Can I undo AI changes?**
A: Since changes are auto-saved immediately, you can manually revert by re-editing the content or using the AI "Tidy & Improve" feature to regenerate. A proper undo/redo feature is coming soon (Cmd/Ctrl+Z).

**Q: What if AI generates wrong information?**
A: Always review and edit AI-generated content. The AI uses your profile data but may need adjustments.

### Suggestions

**Q: Why don't I see suggestions?**
A: Make sure you've clicked on a block to select it. Suggestions only appear for selected blocks.

**Q: Can I turn off suggestions?**
A: Dismiss individual suggestions with the X button. They won't appear again.

**Q: Are suggestions always correct?**
A: Suggestions are recommendations. Use your judgment on which to apply.

### Onboarding

**Q: How do I restart the tutorial?**
A: Clear browser data or look for "Reset Tutorial" in settings (if available).

**Q: Can I skip the tutorial?**
A: Yes, click "Skip tutorial" at any step.

### Export & Sharing

**Q: What format can I export?**
A: PDF format, optimized for ATS systems.

**Q: Can I share my resume with others?**
A: Use the export function and share the PDF file.

**Q: Will the PDF look like the preview?**
A: Yes, the PDF matches your preview exactly.
